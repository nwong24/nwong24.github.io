### What are my interests in software engineering
I entered this type of profession path because I was interested in making more money, but as I spent more time studying I became fascinated with code. Building something from scratch with just my fingertips on a keyboard felt really powerful. After ICS212, I fell in love with the language of C and hope to be able to write code with it as a career. I heard that this type of language is reserved for systems programmers and as such that is the career I aspire to have after graduating.

### My vision skills
I want to be able to improve my skills in C and also gain a more in-depth understanding of how operating systems work under the hood. I also want to learn about how web development works as it seems that many programmers go into that field. I would like to learn more about HTML, CSS, Javascript, Typescript, and the various other web frameworks used by companies to build websites.

### Experiences
I don't have too much formal experience outside of school. I hope to be able to land an internship with a company by next summer, and maybe another one the summer after that. I might even look for an on campus job related to software engineering as preparation for getting a job after I graduate.
